Who we are?

We are 3 Engineering Students who are studying in 3rd Year at Thakur College of Engineering and E-Court was developed by us as part of a project.

Who do we do?

We Help Clients to find their potential lawyers on just a click of a mouse!. We also help Lawyers by allowing them to have a wider reach.

Why do we do so.

We at Hopegivers want to make sure that the Interactions between Lawyers and Clients is as professional as possible. Also we want to make the court case management system more efficient so that it is easy for everyone involved.
